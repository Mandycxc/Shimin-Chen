Lost














Description


This is a group project corroborate with Jiatao Xu. Jiatao created the player control, game obstacles, and coding. I created the scene, characters, and objects.
The game is about a stray kitten got separated from her mother when her mother was out for food. After 3 days, her mother didn’t come back, so the kitten decided to go out and look for her mother. The player will be the kitten walking in the city and the player will face some dangers, like cars, other big cats, humans, and hunger. The goal is to find the kitten’s mom. childhood.
