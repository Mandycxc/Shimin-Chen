Description


This is a group project corroborate with Winnie Wu. Winnie created the Fairy wheel, seesaw and swing. I created the park landscape and responsible for the coding for three.js.
The purpose of the space is to heal people who have been traumatized during their childhood. During the progress of growing up, we had face many obstacles and changes we can’t control. As a kid, we can’t do much but to follow what’s been given to us. The visitors will enter the tunnel at the starting point and as they walk through the darkness and reach the colorful space, they will feel like a process of rebirth. We hope that the visitors find their peace of mind in the space and make up the gaps in childhood.
